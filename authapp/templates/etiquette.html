A WORM WELCOME

Worm Books is a site for readers to discover their next read, as well as share their own recommendations, connecting with a community of readers all around the world.

To ensure that our goal is achieved, there are a few ethical guidelines to keep in mind while online to preserve the quality and integrity of our community, as well as your own overall experience, as you are a vital building block of this community.

RULES
  • -Interact

    Find a book interesting? Leave a like and comment. Hopefully this encourages the next lucky reader to pick it up. ;)

  • -Keep it healthy

    Keep your comments friendly, and criticisms, if you must, creative. ;)

  • -Recommend(optional)

    Don't shy away from sharing that book, or books. ;)

  • -Contribute(optional)

    Some readers may post books with incomplete information. You can help out by filling in the blanks in the contribute section. ;)

  • -Be cool(*please, **required)

    Don't spam. ;)
